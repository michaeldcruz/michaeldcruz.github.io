# About

I'm Mike Cruz, co-founder and CTO of [Always Coach](https://alwayscoach.com).

We run a "dark factory" for software: a delivery line where autonomous agents
take an issue from spec to a tested, deployed production change, and the people
move up the stack to decide what's worth building and whether it turned out well.
Agents ship the safe majority of our roadmap on their own and stop to ask a human
before touching the things that should never be touched blindly: auth, billing,
and database migrations.

This site is where I write about how that actually works, honestly, including the
parts that broke. I also help other companies make the transition, in order, so
the autonomy they end up with is the kind they can trust.

Reach me at [mike@protege.app](mailto:mike@protege.app).
